Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Lexa.

Lexa achieves 99.2% accuracy on average across all supported languages. Our neural translation engine is continuously trained on industry-specific datasets and learns from user corrections to improve over time. For critical translations, we recommend using our human review feature.

Lexa supports all major localization formats including JSON, YAML, XML, PO/POT, XLIFF, Android XML, iOS Strings, ARB (Flutter), and CSV. We also support importing from Figma designs directly and exporting to any format you need.

Absolutely! Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. You can also use our free tier indefinitely which includes 5,000 words per month and 3 languages.

Our REST API allows you to integrate translations directly into your development workflow. We provide SDKs for JavaScript, Python, Ruby, Go, and Java. You can also use our CLI tool to sync translations with your codebase or set up webhooks to automate your localization pipeline.

Security is our top priority. Lexa is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption. We never use your content to train our models without explicit consent, and you can request complete data deletion at any time.
